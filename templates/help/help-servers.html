What is the XBMC Server?

You should think of a XBMC Server as your HTPC where XBMC is installed. Your XBMC Library holds most of the information Maraschino requires to function correctly.
Maraschino allows mutliple Servers, but for the purposes of this help file, we'll assume you only have one.

The first thing you need to do is specify the address where XBMC is installed.
Follow step one on the first page by clicking the link -


Then, fill in the details that correspond to your XBMC setup -

These details can be found in XBMC under Settings > Services > Webserver. Username and Password is optional.
MAC Address is used for WOL (Wake-Up On LAN).
Position is the placement in the list, if you have more than one XBMC Server.

The click Save - You've successfully added a server.
